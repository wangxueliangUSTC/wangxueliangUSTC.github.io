About Me

I am currently a Senior Algorithm Engineer of Kuaishou Technology, where I am mainly responsible for the ranking model and reinforcement learning in recommend systems. Before joining Kuaishou, I was an intern (滴滴新锐计划) at DiDi AI Labs (Jun. 2020 - Jun. 2021, Dec. 2017 - Jun. 2018).

I received my M.S. degree from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2021. I am a member of Laboratory of Machine Intelligence Research and Applications (MIRA), and my advisor is professor Feng Wu and Jie Wang. Before that, I received my B.S. degree in School of Electronic Engineering from XIDIAN UNIVERSITY in June 2018.
